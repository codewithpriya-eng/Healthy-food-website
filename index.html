Why Healthy Food Matters

Healthy food provides essential nutrients that help our body grow, stay active, and fight diseases. A balanced diet includes fruits, vegetables, grains, proteins, and dairy products.

Eating healthy food regularly improves energy levels, strengthens the immune system, and supports overall well-being. It also helps maintain a healthy weight and reduces the risk of many health problems.
