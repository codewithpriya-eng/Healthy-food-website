Essential Nutrients for a Healthy Life

A balanced diet provides all the nutrients required for good health. Each nutrient has a unique role in keeping our body active, strong, and protected from diseases.

🍞 Carbohydrates

Carbohydrates are the body's main source of energy. They help us perform daily activities and keep our muscles active. Rice, bread, potatoes, and cereals are rich in carbohydrates.

🥚 Proteins

Proteins help build and repair body tissues, support muscle growth, and strengthen the immune system. Eggs, fish, milk, beans, and nuts are excellent protein sources.

🥕 Vitamins

Vitamins help improve eyesight, maintain healthy skin, strengthen immunity, and support overall body functions. Fruits and vegetables are rich in vitamins.

🥛 Minerals

Minerals such as calcium and iron are important for strong bones, healthy teeth, and proper blood circulation. Milk, spinach, and nuts are good sources of minerals.
